About me

My name is Alexis Boussaroque, I am 20 years old, and a student at l’Ecole des Mines de Saint-Etienne, a top french engineering school. I relentlessy studied maths and physics for 2 years in a preparatory class to integrate it, but I still love these subjects!

I have always wanted to get involved in state-of-the-art technology, that is why I chose to specialise in computer science and microelectronics. I feel motivated by knowing that my contribution will be useful to other people, and am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow.

I remain curious about all sorts of things, because I believe that understanding the work of others plays a major role in the success of our own.

In the future, I want to bring together my skills and my passions: working on electronics that will be sent to space, designing a new car embedded system, coding a modern AI...

I am currently looking forward to buiding up experience, and discovering work environments abroad.
